REQUIREMENTS FOR STAFF

Universal:
1. Able to start on a date no later than general orientation. For 2017 this is June 18, 2017.
2. Able to work a minimum of 6 weeks with no significant interruptions. Staff end dates are August 1st or August 24th for 2017.
3. Interested and able to live on our camp in Honesdale, PA for the time at camp.

  • Counselors:
    1. 20 years of age or older.
    2. Has significant, well demonstrated experience in working with special needs populations.
    3. Is willing to live at camp, in a bunk, with campers.
    4. Demonstrates a skill set in leadership and/or child management techniques.

  • Specialists:
    1. 19 years of age or older.
    2. Has a specific skill area in which they have formerly led others, not simply practiced themselves.
    3. Is willing to live at camp, in a bunk, with campers.

  • Nurse/Health Center:
    1. Certified RN or LPN.
    2. Experience with pediatric, disability, and/or at risk populations.
    3. Able and willing to live on camp in health center.
